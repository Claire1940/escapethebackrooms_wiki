### How to Join a Cross-Platform Game

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Cross-Platform Matchmaking">
    Access the game settings and ensure cross-platform matchmaking is enabled. A warning will display if it is active.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Create or Join a Lobby">
    One player creates a lobby (public or private). A unique **lobby code** will be generated and displayed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Share the Lobby Code">
    Share the generated lobby code with your friends. They can enter this code to join your game. There's an option to hide the code for content creators to prevent unwanted joiners.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Communicate and Play">
    Enjoy playing with your cross-platform team! Voice chat is fully functional across all supported platforms.
  </Step>
</Steps>
