### Overcoming Control and Item Bugs
Players often encounter issues with controller tracking and item functionality in VR.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Recalibrate Controllers">
    If your controller position is tracked incorrectly, **recenter your VR headset and controllers** frequently. This can often resolve temporary tracking glitches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Be Mindful of Interactables">
    When using interactive objects or hiding, your head will be locked in place. **Plan your movements carefully** around entities, as this restriction can leave you vulnerable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Adjust Item Usage">
    Items like Chainsaws, Walkie Talkies, and Bug Spray can cause your virtual hands to "break." While usable, be aware of this visual bug and **practice using them in safer areas** if possible. Aiming fireworks without a crosshair requires more intuitive aiming.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
The "broken hands" visual bug with certain items is a known issue in the VR mode. While it doesn't always prevent functionality, it can be disorienting.
</Callout>
