<Section icon={Target} title="Setup Guide: Achieving 120 FPS">

To get the most out of **Escape the Backrooms android**, you must configure the Lyfb Frame Generator correctly. This tool works by taking the base frames rendered by the emulator and generating intermediate frames to smooth out the motion, effectively doubling or quadrupling the perceived frame rate without doubling the GPU load.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Root and Install Lyfb">
    Ensure your device is rooted and install the Lyfb Frame Generator from the Play Store. Flash the required module via your root manager and grant the app superuser permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Configure Emulator Settings">
    Inside Winlator or GameHub, disable **Force Native Rendering**. This allows the external frame generator to intercept the video stream properly. Set your internal game resolution to **720p or 900p** for the best performance-to-clarity ratio.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Lyfb Presets">
    Open the Lyfb app and select the **Clarity Preferred** mode. Set the frame limiter in the game to **30 FPS** and set the Lyfb output to **4x** to achieve a smooth **120 FPS** display output.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Thermal Management">
    Enable **Bypass Charging** if your device supports it. This prevents the battery from heating up during intense sessions, allowing the CPU to maintain high clock speeds without thermal throttling.
  </Step>
</Steps>
