<Section icon={Target} title="Level-by-Level Escape Guide for Escape the Backrooms">
Each level in Escape the Backrooms presents distinct challenges and entities. A structured approach to each one can significantly improve your chances of escape.

### Level 0: The Lobby
This is your first trial, a seemingly endless maze of faded yellow walls. Your objective is to find a broken ladder, repair it, obtain a key, and unlock the exit. The Howler entity will appear after a few minutes, giving chase.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Ladder Pieces">
    Wander through the winding halls until you find the scattered pieces of a ladder. Collect them to repair it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Obtain the Key">
    Use the repaired ladder to reach a cubby where the key is located.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Find the Locked Door">
    Search for the locked door, which often leads to a room with square pitfalls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Cross the Pitfalls">
    Carefully cross the pitfalls without losing your balance. Falling will lead you to a second layer with a separate exit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Beware The Howler">
The Howler will begin appearing on the map after a few minutes. It can spot you through walls if not contained properly (as seen in out-of-bounds observations). Maintain distance and use hiding spots.
</Callout>

### Level 1: The Habitable Zone
This level features a large parking lot and introduces the Skin-Stealer. You'll need to find a code for an elevator and collect four keys to open a vault door.

| Phase                | Objective                                      | Entity               | Key Tip                                                              |
| :------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- | :------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Parking Garage**   | Find the elevator code (from colored cars).    | None (initially)     | Note car positions; they indicate the code.                          |
| **Down the Elevator**| Collect **4 keys** scattered in rooms.         | **Skin-Stealer**     | Skin-Stealers cannot open doors; use them to your advantage for evasion. |
| **The Dark Garage**  | Navigate to the next elevator.                 | **Skin-Stealer**     | Darkness makes detection hard; listen for sounds.                    |

### Hiding from Entities
Most entities in the Backrooms can be evaded by using the environment to your advantage.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Closets & Under Tables">
    These are universal hiding spots. When a monster gives chase, quickly duck into a closet or under a table to break their line of sight.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Closing Doors">
    Many entities cannot open doors. Closing doors behind you can buy precious seconds or even cause the entity to despawn if they lose track of you.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="success" title="Multiplayer Advantage">
Playing with friends can significantly ease the difficulty. One player can distract an entity while others complete objectives, or revive fallen teammates if equipped with a rope in Level 0.
</Callout>

### Level Progression Flow
The game features a semi-linear progression, often looping back to "The Hub" or introducing alternative paths.

| Level Name             | Primary Objective                                   | Connection Points                                       |
| :--------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Level 0 (The Lobby)** | Find key, open exit.                                | Leads to Level 1.                                       |
| **Level 1 (Habitable Zone)** | Find elevator code, collect keys, open vault.       | Leads to The Hub.                                       |
| **The Hub**            | Access M.E.G. base, open garage door.               | Leads to Pipe Dreams 1 (Exit A) or Level 922 (Exit B).  |
| **Electrical Station** | Activate 9 fuse boxes.                              | Leads to Abandoned Office.                              |
| **Abandoned Office**   | Solve vending machine puzzle, avoid cameras.        | Leads to Terror Hotel.                                  |
| **Terror Hotel**       | Obtain Moth Jelly, collect papers, solve padlock.   | Leads to Pipe Dreams 2.                                 |
| **Pipe Dreams 2**      | Navigate tunnel, escape Smiler chase.               | Leads to Level Fun.                                     |
| **Level Fun**          | Sneak past Partygoers.                              | Leads to Poolrooms.                                     |
| **Poolrooms**          | Explore corridors, find exit.                       | Leads to Run For Your Life.                             |
| **Run For Your Life**  | Sprint through hospital hallway, dodge obstacles.   | Leads to Level The End.                                 |
| **Level The End**      | Collect VHS tapes, use computer.                    | Leads to Escherian Stairwell.                           |
| **Escherian Stairwell**| Climb to floor 94, jump down center.                | Leads to Level 94: Motion.                              |
| **Level 94: Motion**   | Reach floating castle, defeat Animated King.        | Leads to Lights Out.                                    |
| **Lights Out**         | Navigate dark maze with LIDAR scanner.              | Leads to Thalassophobia (Exit A) or Kitty's House (Exit B). |
| **Thalassophobia**     | Traverse flooded village, dive into cave system.    | Leads to Level 8.                                       |
| **Level 8**            | Navigate moth caves, avoid Skin-Stealers.           | Leads to Level 0.11.                                    |
| **Level 0.11 & 188**   | Solve light puzzle, use chainsaw, pull levers.      | Leads to Level 9.                                       |
| **Level 9**            | Activate security terminals, cure Wretch.           | Leads to Level 10.                                      |
| **Level 10**           | Navigate wheatfields, activate turbines.            | Leads to Level 3999 (True Ending).                      |
