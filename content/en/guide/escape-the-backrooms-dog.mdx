These creatures are designed to instill fear and pressure, often appearing in tight corridors or open areas, forcing players to think quickly and react decisively. <Callout type="tip" title="Flashlight Mechanics">
The flashlight acts as a deterrent, not a weapon. Shining it on a Hound can make it flinch and run away. On Normal or Easy difficulty, you might need to flash it up to three times. On Hard or Nightmare, it often only works once, making timing and positioning crucial.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Evasion and Deterrence Strategies">
Dealing with the **Escape the Backrooms dog** effectively involves a combination of flashlight usage, environmental awareness, and careful movement. Simply running away might not always be the best option, especially if you get cornered. Understanding when to confront and when to avoid is key to survival.

Different Hounds may exhibit slightly different behaviors. Some might despawn after a few successful flashes, while others might just retreat to another area, only to return later. It's important to observe their reactions and adapt your strategy accordingly.

| Strategy          | Description                                                                                             | Effectiveness (Normal/Hard) | Risk Level |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :-------------------------- | :--------- |
| **Flashlight Stun** | Shine your flashlight directly at the Hound to make it flinch and run.                                  | High/Medium                 | Low/Medium |
| **Door Technique**  | Hide behind a door and flash the Hound as it approaches. The light can pass through, scaring it away.   | High                        | Low        |
| **Environmental Evasion** | Utilize obstacles, tight spaces, or electric puddles (if applicable) to create distance.             | Medium                      | Medium     |
| **Sanity Management** | Keep your sanity high with Almond Water; low sanity can make you more vulnerable and disoriented.      | High                        | Low        |
| **Controlled Retreat** | If a Hound is persistent, strategically retreat to a safer area, keeping it in view if possible.        | Medium                      | Medium     |

<Callout type="warning" title="Electric Puddles">
On certain levels, like the latter parts of Level 3, electric puddles can be fatal. Be extremely careful not to step into them while evading Hounds, as this will result in immediate death. Always prioritize safe footing over chasing a fleeing Hound.
</Callout>

</Section>

| :---------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |

| **Open Rooms**    | More space for evasion, but Hounds might circle back.                       | Maintain distance, use flashlight, and utilize cover if available.              |
| **Crawl Spaces**  | Generally safe from Hounds, but can be bottlenecks.                         | Use to escape, but be aware of Hounds waiting at exits.                         |

  <Step num="3" title="Evasion Route">
