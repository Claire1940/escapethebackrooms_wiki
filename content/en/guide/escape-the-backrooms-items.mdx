<Section icon={Wrench} title="Utility Tools and Level-Specific Equipment">

Some items in the Backrooms are not meant for consumption but are required to interact with the environment or solve puzzles. These utility **escape the backrooms items** often have unlimited uses but occupy valuable inventory space.

The Crowbar, for instance, is essential in the Office level. It allows players to reset security cameras by interacting with vents outside the security room. Meanwhile, the Lidar Scanner is the only way to navigate the absolute darkness of Level 6. It features two modes: motion detection for tracking entities and dot projection for mapping the physical layout of the room.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Bug Spray">
    Find Bug Spray in Level 5 or Level 9. It has unlimited uses and is required for harvesting resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Male Death Moths">
    Locate Male Death Moths (usually found near light sources). Do not confuse them with the much larger and deadlier Female Death Moths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Harvest Moth Jelly">
    Spray the Male Death Moth to kill it instantly. Interact with the remains to collect Moth Jelly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate Boiler Room">
    Use the Thermometer to monitor heat levels. Rising temperatures indicate a Female Death Moth is nearby; use this to avoid detection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Tool | Level Found | Specific Use | Mechanic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Crowbar** | Level 4 / Hub | Reset cameras / Open vents | Environmental Interaction |
| **Lidar Scanner**| Level 6 | Navigation in total darkness | Dot projection / Motion sensor |
| **Thermometer** | Boiler Room | Detect Female Death Moths | Heat sensing |
| **Bug Spray** | Level 5 / Level 9 | Kill Male Death Moths | Chemical projectile |
