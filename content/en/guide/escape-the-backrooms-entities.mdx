<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Evasion Techniques">
Basic avoidance might get you through the initial levels, but as you delve deeper into the Backrooms, the entities become more cunning. Mastering advanced evasion techniques is crucial for long-term survival.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Mastering the Environment">
    Utilize the unique layouts of each level. Some areas offer more cover, while others have environmental hazards that can be used against entities. Learn to recognize these features quickly. **Broken walls** or **unstable flooring** can sometimes create temporary barriers or distractions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Distraction and Decoy">
    Many entities react to sound. Carrying **throwable objects** can create diversions, drawing an entity's attention away from your intended path. This is especially effective against blind or sound-sensitive entities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Light Management">
    Light can be both a friend and a foe. While a flashlight helps you see, it can also reveal your position. Learn when to use it sparingly and when to **extinguish it completely** to blend into the darkness, especially against sight-based entities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Understanding Entity Weaknesses">
    Some entities have specific weaknesses. While direct combat is rarely an option, understanding what deters an entity (e.g., bright lights, specific sounds, certain environmental conditions) can be invaluable for survival. This knowledge often comes from **observing their behavior** during initial encounters.
  </Step>
</Steps>
