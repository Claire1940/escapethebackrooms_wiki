### Initial Levels and Their Challenges

The early levels introduce players to core mechanics and common threats. These often serve as a tutorial, albeit a brutal one, to the unsettling nature of the Backrooms.

| Level | Description | Key Challenges | Entities |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Level 0** | The "Lobby," an endless maze of yellow wallpaper and fluorescent lights. | Navigating the maze, maintaining sanity. | None initially, but the pervasive sense of dread is a constant threat. |
| **Level 1** | A concrete parking garage-like environment with industrial lighting. | Collecting keys, avoiding **Skin-Stealers** and **Smilers**. | Skin-Stealers, Smilers |
| **Level 2** | A dark, pipe-filled maintenance tunnel. | A "walking simulator" segment that can be tedious, requiring multiple passes. | None initially, focus on navigation. |

<Section icon={Target} title="Navigating Mid-Game Levels and Their Unique Mechanics">
As you delve deeper into the Backrooms, the levels become more complex, introducing unique gameplay mechanics and a wider array of entities. These levels often require more strategic thinking and careful execution.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Survive Level 5 (The Terror Hotel)">
    This level is divided into sections. The first involves killing bugs and collecting "moth jelly" for keys with no immediate danger. The second section is significantly harder, featuring a scary environment with challenging puzzles and potentially dangerous entities. Cheating by looking up door codes online can simplify it, but it's more rewarding to solve it yourself.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Conquer Level 6 (Lights Out)">
    Level 6 starts as a simple labyrinth. However, it quickly transitions into a dark labyrinth where a **Wretch** actively hunts you. The key is to navigate quietly and efficiently, using the environment to your advantage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Level 10 (A Field of Wheat)">
    Often hated, but manageable if you understand its mechanics. This level involves navigating a vast wheat field while avoiding **Facelings**. Knowing their patrol patterns and how to effectively hide is crucial for a smooth passage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Explore Level 94 (The Floating Castle)">
    This level features a unique day-night cycle. During the day, you can walk freely. At night, you must hide from unseen threats. It's a simple concept but effectively ramps up the tension and difficulty. There's also a floating castle section that is both scary and difficult.
  </Step>
</Steps>
