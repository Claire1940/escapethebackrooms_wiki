### Level 0: The Start of the Nightmare

Level 0 is your entry point into the Backrooms, a seemingly endless expanse of yellow wallpaper and humming fluorescent lights. Survival here involves finding a key and unlocking an exit while managing your sanity.

<Callout type="tip">
To earn an achievement, survive in Level 0 for 15 minutes. This is a great opportunity to get a feel for the game's atmosphere without immediate threat.
</Callout>

**Level 0 Objectives:**
| Objective | Description | Key Item/Action |
|:----------|:------------|:----------------|
| Find Locked Door | Locate the main exit door. | N/A |
| Obtain Key | Search for the key in a nearby cubby. | Collect ladder pieces |
| Unlock Door | Use the key to open the main exit. | Key |
| Navigate Square Holes | Cross the room with square holes. | Multiplayer ropes / Alternate exit arrows |

### The Habitable Zone & Parking Garage

Following Level 0, you'll encounter The Habitable Zone and the multi-part Parking Garage. These levels introduce more complex puzzles and the first aggressive entities.

<Callout type="warning" title="Skin Stealers Ahead">
The Parking Garage features Skin Stealers. They cannot open doors, so use this to your advantage by closing doors behind you to escape.
</Callout>

**Parking Garage Key Steps:**
<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Find Elevator Code">
    Locate the code for the elevator, often determined by the position of colored cars.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect 4 Keys">
    In the "Down the Elevator" section, find four keys scattered in rooms. Be wary of Skin Stealers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Central Door">
    Turn all four keys simultaneously in the central door. Timing is crucial as they reset if you take too long.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate Dark Garage">
    In the "Dark Garage," locate the exit door to the next level while evading Skin Stealers in the low light.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Unlocking the Endings

Escape the Backrooms features multiple endings, each requiring specific actions or routes.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Ending A: The Elevator">
    Reach Level 55.1 and complete the photography objectives on three different floors. This is considered the "normal" ending.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ending B: The Grassrooms">
    Navigate the Overgrowth and Grassrooms, solving environmental puzzles to reach this ending. Requires careful movement on grass.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ending C: Level 922">
    From Level 1, take a specific M.E.G. door to Level 922. Climb 94 or more levels and jump down the staircase.
  </Card>
  <Card title="The True Ending">
    Collect endings A, B, and C. Then, return to the M.E.G. Base elevator, go to Level 3999, and interact with the arcade machine.
  </Card>

### Level 37.2: The Poolrooms and Stagnant Pools

This sprawling water-based level presents unique environmental challenges and new types of dangers.

**Poolrooms Navigation:**
| Section                   | Primary Objective               | Key Mechanic / Threat       |
|:--------------------------|:--------------------------------|:----------------------------|
| Poolrooms Section 1       | Maneuver safety raft, raise water | Bacteria in water, valve puzzles |
| Stagnant Pools Section 1  | Turn 4 valves, fill tank        | Clump patrols between valves |
| Poolrooms Section 2       | Turn 8 valves, cross broken bridge | Timer for valves, pit below bridge |
| Stagnant Pools Section 2  | Turn 3 valves, drain water      | Parasites in platforming sections |
| Stagnant Pools Section 3  | Turn 7 valves                   | Large area, monster patrols |

<Callout type="warning" title="Beware the Bacteria">
In Level 37.2, falling into the water where bacteria is present will result in a quick death. Stay on your raft or designated safe areas.
</Callout>
