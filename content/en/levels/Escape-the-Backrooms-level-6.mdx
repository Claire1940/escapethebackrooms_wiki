<Section icon={Shield} title="Evading the Wretch: Your Survival Strategy">
The Wretch is the sole entity you'll encounter in **Escape the Backrooms Level 6**, but it is a formidable one. It is completely blind, relying on sound to track its prey. This characteristic dictates your entire survival strategy within the level. Your best defense is to remain silent and unseen.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Looting">
    Upon spawning, check both rooms adjacent to your starting point for essential items. These can include sanity-boosting items or other consumables.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate LiDAR Scanners">
    Systematically follow the hallways. Your goal is to find a LiDAR scanner. These are critical for navigating the dark.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip and Use LiDAR">
    Once a LiDAR scanner is found, equip it immediately. This will provide you with a visual representation of your surroundings, though it's still challenging.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Crouch to Evade">
    Whenever you detect the Wretch nearby, immediately crouch. While crouched, the Wretch cannot hear you and will not attack, unless it saw you enter the crouched state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Maintain Sanity">
    Avoid rushing. If you have enough items to keep your sanity stable, take your time. Rushing increases the risk of making noise and alerting the Wretch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Wretch Detection">
Listen carefully for the Wretch's distinct sounds. The moment you hear it, prepare to crouch. Early detection is key to avoiding a fatal encounter. Remember, it cannot hear you while you're already crouched, but if it sees you crouch, it will still attack.
</Callout>

Understanding the Wretch's limitations is crucial. It cannot hear you or kill you while you are crouched, provided it didn't see you crouch down. This makes strategic crouching the cornerstone of your evasion tactics. Moving slowly and deliberately, especially when the Wretch is in the vicinity, will significantly increase your chances of survival.

</Section>
