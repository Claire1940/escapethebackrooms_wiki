<Section icon={Target} title="East Substation: Initial Power-Up">
The East Substation is your starting point. You'll find a panel with nine lights, indicating the status of all switches. Three of these lights correspond to this section. The switch locations are randomized across three possible spots.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the First Switch Cluster">
    From the main panel, head right and unlock the first door. Proceed through the corridor. The first possible switch location is on the wall just inside the first door you open. If it's not there, continue down the corridor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Subsequent Locations">
    Further down the corridor, you'll find a second possible switch location. Activate any switches you find. A third potential spot is through another door in this area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Explore the Final East Section">
    Continue down the corridor to the final set of three possible locations. These are typically found on walls to the left or right, or at the very end of the path. Flick all three switches for this section.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Collect Almond Water">
Almond Water is crucial for sanity. The East Substation often has bottles on shelves. Always grab them, even if your sanity is full, as you'll need them for later sections.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Central Substation: Encountering the 'Dog Things'">
After activating the East Substation switches, the gate to the Central Substation will open. This is where you'll face your first hostile entity: the "dog thing." These creatures are afraid of light, making your torch your primary defense.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter the Central Substation">
    Proceed through the now-open gate. Stock up on Almond Water and torches if needed, as they respawn in this area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="First Dog Encounter and Switch 1">
    Your first switch is often on a wall early in this section. Soon after, you'll encounter a "dog thing." Shine your torch on it three times to make it flinch and run away. Some dogs despawn after this, others simply flee.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate Remaining Switches">
    Continue through the halls, checking walls and side rooms. Possible locations include walls in open areas, behind doors, and in small alcoves. Use the "door technique" (hiding behind a door and shining your torch through it) if a dog gets too close.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Torch Usage">
Your torch is a weapon. Shine it on a "dog thing" three times to scare it away. Be quick, as they can still attack if they get too close while you're activating a switch.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="West Substation: Electric Puddles & Advanced Evasion">
The West Substation is the most challenging part of Level 3. It introduces electric puddles, which are instantly fatal, and more aggressive "dog things" that require precise evasion.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Dog Evasion & First Switch">
    Upon entering, a "dog thing" will likely be visible. Make sure it sees you, then scare it away with your torch. Immediately run to the left door; do not go right, as another dog may ambush you. The first switch is often in this left area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Laser Puzzle Switches">
    Cross the hall to find another switch location, often involving a laser puzzle. Rotate the pieces until the lasers align with the colors. You'll need to dodge the dog again after this.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Crawl Spaces and Final Switches">
    Navigate through crawl spaces and open doors. Be extremely cautious of electric puddles. Some dogs in crawl spaces despawn after being scared. Look for switches on walls within these tight areas and in small side rooms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Electric Puddle Danger">
Stepping into an electric puddle will result in instant death. Always scan the ground before moving, especially in dimly lit areas.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Escape the Backrooms Level 3: Final Steps">
Once all nine switches across the three substations are activated, you'll hear a distinct sound indicating the final exit gate has opened. Now, your goal is to retrace your steps back to the beginning of the level and escape.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Return to the Start">
    Head back through the West and Central Substations, using the same evasion tactics for the "dog things." The dogs you previously scared away may reappear, so stay vigilant.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate Safely">
    Remember the layout and the locations of electric puddles. Your sanity will continue to drain, so if you have any Almond Water left, use it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Exit Level 3">
    Once you reach the initial panel where you started, the main exit gate will be open. Proceed through it to complete Level 3.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Hard Mode Considerations">
On Hard or Nightmare difficulties, "dog things" are more aggressive, and the door technique becomes even more critical. Direct confrontation with the torch might be less effective, requiring more strategic hiding and movement.
</Callout>
