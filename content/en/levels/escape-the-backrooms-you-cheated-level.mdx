<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Access the 'You Cheated' Level">
Accessing the "You Cheated" level is not straightforward and involves specific actions within Level 188. While its name suggests cheating, there's actually a legitimate, albeit tricky, way to get there without using external cheats.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 188">
    Progress through *Escape the Backrooms* until you reach **Level 188**. This is a prerequisite for accessing the secret level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock the Metal Door">
    In Level 188, you need to find and place all **three TVs**. This action will unlock a specific metal door.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate Past Aranea Membri">
    Beyond the metal door, you'll encounter the **Aranea Membri** guarding a set of wooden doors. This is the critical part where you can either "cheat" or use a specific exploit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Exploit the Aranea Membri (Non-Cheating Method)">
    To access the "You Cheated" level legitimately, hide behind the glass panels in the exit room of Level 188. Wait for the Aranea Membri to move to the opposite side of the room. Once it's sufficiently far, make a quick dash for the wooden doors. Position yourself as close to the doors as possible before making your move to minimize the risk of being caught.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Enter the Doors">
    Successfully passing the Aranea Membri and entering the wooden doors will transport you directly into the **"You Cheated" level**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Access Method         | Description                                                                                                                              | Difficulty | Intended Outcome                                       |
| :-------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :--------- | :----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Legitimate Exploit** | Utilizing environmental features (glass panels) and enemy AI patterns to bypass the Aranea Membri without triggering it.                 | High       | Access the level as a hidden secret.                   |
| **Actual Cheating**    | Using external tools or commands to teleport or clip through boundaries, directly bypassing the Aranea Membri and entering the door. | Low        | Access the level by breaking game rules.               |

<Callout type="info" title="Return Path">
To exit the "You Cheated" level, simply use the corridor behind you. This will return you to **Level 188**, allowing you to continue your normal progression.
</Callout>
