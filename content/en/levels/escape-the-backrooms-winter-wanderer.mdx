<Section icon={Swords} title="Nightmare Mode Overhaul">
For those seeking the ultimate challenge, the Winter Wanderer update brings significant changes to Nightmare Mode. Previously, some players noted inconsistencies, but the 1.1 patch ensures the mode now works as intended, delivering a truly unforgiving experience.

The core change to Nightmare Mode is the complete removal of checkpoints and save files. This means that if any player dies at any point during a playthrough, all players must restart from the very beginning. This heightened stakes approach demands flawless execution and impeccable teamwork, transforming the mode into a true test of skill and resilience.

| Feature         | Old Nightmare Mode      | New Nightmare Mode (1.1)    | Impact on Gameplay                                     |
| :-------------- | :----------------------- | :-------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------- |
| Checkpoints     | Accessible               | **Removed**                 | Increases difficulty, requires full run completion.     |
| Save Files      | Accessible               | **Removed**                 | No mid-run saves, all or nothing.                      |
| Player Death    | Individual restart/respawn | **All players restart**     | Emphasizes teamwork, one mistake can end the run.      |
| Difficulty      | High                     | **Extremely High**          | Designed for expert players and coordinated teams.     |
| Reward          | Standard                 | **Enhanced sense of achievement** | Victory is hard-earned and incredibly satisfying.      |

This adjustment pushes players to their limits, making every decision and every step critical. It's an experience designed for those who truly wish to conquer the Backrooms without a safety net.
