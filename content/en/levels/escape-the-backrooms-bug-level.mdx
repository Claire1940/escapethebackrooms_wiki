### Room 1: The Painting Puzzle and Moth Jelly

The first room introduces you to the level's mechanics. You'll need to solve a painting puzzle and collect Moth Jelly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Bug Spray">
    Upon spawning, locate the desk and equip the Bug Spray. This item is crucial for interacting with the small moth swarms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Solve the Painting Puzzle">
    Identify four paintings on the walls, each with an arrow underneath. Click them in order from the youngest to the oldest person depicted:
    1.  Girl with two hair loops
    2.  Man with a fancy suit and serious face
    3.  Man with a fancy suit looking directly at the viewer
    4.  Man with a wrinkled face
    Solving this puzzle will open a door.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect Moth Jelly">
    Use the Bug Spray on a small moth swarm to obtain Moth Jelly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Retrieve the Key">
    Place the Moth Jelly in a wooden box located at the end of the hall on the left. This action will grant you a key.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unlock the Door">
    Use the key to unlock the door and proceed to the next room.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Giant Moth Avoidance Tactics

The key to surviving Room 3 lies in understanding and exploiting the giant moths' limitations.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor Temperature with Thermometer">
    Always have your thermometer equipped. A sudden jump to 100°F or higher indicates a giant moth is in your immediate vicinity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Crouch Immediately">
    If the temperature rises or your screen blurs (a sign of an approaching moth), immediately crouch. Giant moths cannot harm you while you are in a crouched position. Maintain crouching until you are out of the danger zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Turn Off Flashlight">
    It is generally safer to keep your flashlight off in areas with moths, as light can attract them. If your screen blurs, definitely turn it off.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Red Valves">
    If a moth is chasing you, locate and press a red valve. These valves release heat that blocks moths from advancing, giving you a temporary reprieve or allowing you to escape.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Identify Safe Paths">
    Follow the guidance of pipes and established routes. Getting lost increases your exposure to moths and depletes sanity. A common landmark for correct paths involves following distinct pipe formations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Hive Alert">
Do NOT open any red doors without a green exit sign above them. These doors lead to hives, which are extremely dangerous and will result in immediate death.
</Callout>
