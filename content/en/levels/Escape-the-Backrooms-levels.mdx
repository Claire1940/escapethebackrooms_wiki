<Section icon={Trophy} title="Complex Puzzles and Environmental Challenges">
Later **Escape the Backrooms levels** weave intricate puzzles with environmental hazards, pushing players to their limits. These levels often require collecting specific items or deciphering codes to progress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Abandoned Office: Vending Machine Puzzle">
    In the break room, match the number on vending machines to the count of items displayed on their covers (e.g., tables, coolers, books). This requires careful counting and observation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Terror Hotel: Moth Jelly Collection">
    In the lobby, use bug spray on male Death Moths to collect Moth Jelly. This jelly is used to obtain keys from a dumbwaiter. Be prepared for multiple moth encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Terror Hotel: Room Code Decryption">
    Collect letter-bearing papers from open mailboxes in the hotel rooms. Assemble these letters to form a word, which reveals a numerical code for a locked door. Possible codes include **17564**, **89472**, and **05938**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Service Crawlspace: Moth Evasion">
    Navigate a maze filled with Female Death Moths, using thermometers to detect their heat signatures. Crawl past them to avoid detection, as they are sensitive to motion.
  </Step>
</Steps>
