<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding The Hub: Escape the Backrooms Road Level">
The Hub in *Escape the Backrooms* is a crucial central area, often referred to as the "road level" due to its tunnel-like structure and interconnectivity. This large underground tunnel acts as a nexus, providing access to many other levels within the game. Successfully navigating The Hub is essential for progression, allowing players to move between different zones once their respective doors are unlocked.

The primary objective upon first entering The Hub is to activate the main gate, which then grants access to other areas. This involves interacting with an abandoned M.E.G. Base located within the tunnel.

<Callout type="tip" title="Navigation Tip">
Always be mindful of the doors in The Hub. While they offer access to other levels, they must be unlocked from within those specific levels before you can use them from The Hub. Plan your exploration accordingly.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Objectives and Gate Activation">
Your initial foray into The Hub involves a two-part objective centered around a critical gate. This gate's activation is vital for opening up further exploration opportunities within the Backrooms.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate The M.E.G. Base">
    Upon arriving from Level 1, your first task is to find and enter **The M.E.G. Base** within The Hub. It's typically located on the left side of the main tunnel.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock the Computer">
    Inside The M.E.G. Base, you'll find a computer. To unlock it, input the password "**itheardyou**". This grants you access to its files.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate the Gate">
    Once the computer is unlocked, open the file named "**Gate.exe**". Executing this file will successfully unlock the main gate in The Hub.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Return to Level 1 (Optional)">
    After the gate is unlocked, you can return to Level 1. The previously inaccessible gate there will now be open, allowing for continued progression.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Password Security">
The password "itheardyou" is case-sensitive. Ensure you type it exactly as shown to avoid access issues with the computer in The M.E.G. Base.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Unlocking Level Doors from The Hub">
The Hub features 10 distinct doors, each leading to a different level. However, these doors are not immediately accessible. They must first be unlocked from within their respective levels. This system encourages players to fully explore each level before gaining convenient access back to The Hub.

Here’s a guide to finding and unlocking some key level doors:

| Level Number | Location to Unlock Door | Key Features/Hints |
| :----------- | :---------------------- | :----------------- |
| **Level 0**  | From Level 8, in the smaller area near The Manilla Room. | Go forward/away from The Manilla Room, then slightly right. |
| **Level 1**  | On floor 4 of Level 1. | Clearly noticeable and out in the open; essential for initial progression. |
| **Level 4**  | In the large Pillar Room, to the left. | After the staircase at the start, go through an empty room to reach the Pillar Room. |
| **Level 7**  | Upstairs area, through a specific door. | Go through the door at the top of the initial room, then up another set of stairs. |
| **Level 9**  | Specific house, downstairs stone staircase. | Look for a house with a Wretch guarding the upstairs area. |
| **Level 37** | At the top of a staircase in a large room. | Enter square entrances to find the large room with the staircase. |
| **Level 52** | In the starting room, on the right side. | The door is prominently located on the right. |
| **Level 94** | Upstairs of the very first house. | Open the door in the upstairs room to find The Hub's door. |
| **Level 188**| Through a door labeled "H" in a room with many labeled doors. | Follow the hallway after entering the "H" door. |
| **Level 3999**| Behind a staff-only door on the left at the start. | Go through the sliding doors, then check the left for the staff access. |

<Callout type="info" title="Door Unlocking Mechanism">
Each door in The Hub acts as a one-way portal until it's activated from the destination level. Think of it as establishing a fast-travel point rather than an immediate entry.
</Callout>

**Trivia about The Hub:**
- The Hub boasts the most entrances and exits of any single level in the entire game, making it truly central.
- Interestingly, The Hub does not feature any doors leading to levels without numerical designations or to sublevels.
- The unique ambiance of The Hub was crafted by recording highway sounds, then muffling, slowing, and adding echo effects, complemented by a pitched-down choir from the Kontakt 5 Factory Library.
