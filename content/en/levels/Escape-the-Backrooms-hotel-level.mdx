<Section icon={Target} title="Main Hall Objectives: Unlocking the Elevator">
Your first task in **Escape the Backrooms hotel level** begins in The Main Hall. Here, you'll encounter a locked glass door leading to an elevator, secured by three distinct locks. To obtain the keys, you must engage with the peculiar ecosystem of Male Deathmoths.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Moth Clusters">
    Search The Main Hall for three clusters of **Male Deathmoths**. These are usually found in various branching rooms like the café or The Beverly Room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Bug Spray">
    Use **Bug Spray** on each cluster of Male Deathmoths. They will die and transform into **Moth Jelly**. You'll need three portions of this jelly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Feed the Dumbwaiter">
    Find the **Dumbwaiter** in the left hallway. A note reads, "_iF YOU FEED THEM THEY WiLL REWARD YOU_". Place one Moth Jelly into the Dumbwaiter and send it down. It will return with a **Key**. Repeat this process three times for all three keys.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock the Elevator">
    Apply all three Keys to the locks on the glass door. Once unlocked, a hallway will open, leading to an elevator that transports you to The Hotel floor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Moth Jelly Management">
If you accidentally consume a Moth Jelly, don't panic. Another Male Deathmoth will spawn elsewhere on the floor, allowing you to collect the required amount. Always prioritize obtaining three Moth Jellies.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Hotel Floor: Code Hunting and Entity Evasion">
Upon arriving at The Hotel floor, the atmosphere shifts, and the true terror of the **Escape the Backrooms hotel level** begins. This section requires careful navigation and puzzle-solving while evading dangerous entities.

### Finding the Code for Room 235

| Step | Action                                    | Details                                                                                                          |
|------|-------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1    | Identify Open Mailboxes                   | Locate the Mailbox with compartments for each room. Seven boxes will be open, indicating target rooms.           |
| 2    | Collect Papers                            | Visit the rooms corresponding to the open mailboxes. Find a **paper** on a drawer in each room.                  |
| 3    | Deposit Papers                            | Return to the Mailbox and place each collected paper into its respective compartment.                            |
| 4    | Reveal the Code                           | Once all papers are placed, the phrase "LOOK UP" will appear, and a 5-digit code will be displayed on the ceiling. |
| 5    | Access Room 235                           | Enter the revealed code into the Number Lock on **Room 235**'s door to proceed to The Boiler Room.              |

<Callout type="success" title="Possible Room 235 Codes">
There are only three possible 5-digit codes for Room 235's Number Lock:
- **17564**
- **05938**
- **89472**
</Callout>

### Entity Encounters in The Hotel

The Hotel floor is not deserted. You'll encounter two primary threats:
- **Skin-Stealer (1)**: A formidable entity that can be temporarily evaded by hiding under beds or in closets, though a known bug can cause it to become stuck.
- **Hound (1)**: Another dangerous entity roaming the corridors. Maintain awareness of your surroundings to avoid detection.

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Boiler Room Navigation: Escaping to Level 2">
The Boiler Room is a stark contrast to the previous areas, featuring stone walls, floors, and a labyrinth of pipes. This final section of the **Escape the Backrooms hotel level** is infested with Female Deathmoths and requires precise navigation to find the exit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter The Boiler Room">
    After inputting the code for Room 235, proceed through the door and up the stairs to enter The Boiler Room. You'll find a table with four **Thermometers** and a note at the start.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Detect Danger with Thermometers">
    Equip a Thermometer. If it reads anything other than **72 degrees**, danger is nearby. Warmer areas indicate higher concentrations of **Female Deathmoths** and their nests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Evade Female Deathmoths">
    Navigate carefully, avoiding warm areas and Moth Jelly puddles on the ground. Use **Bug Spray** from valves to scare off Deathmoths if necessary.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locate the True Exit">
    The Boiler Room has four doors, but only one is the exit back to Level 2. Look for the door with an **exit sign** above it. Ignore any purple doors, as these hide Deathmoth Hives.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Boiler Room Checkpoints">
The Boiler Room features three checkpoints strategically placed throughout the level. These provide a much-needed reprieve, allowing you to respawn closer to your progress should you fall victim to the many dangers.
</Callout>
