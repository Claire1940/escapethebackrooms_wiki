<Section icon={Map} title="Navigating the Ocean and Underwater Depths">
The journey through Level 7 is divided into two distinct parts: navigating the surface ocean and exploring the deep underwater sections. Each part presents its own set of challenges and objectives, crucial for successfully escaping the Backrooms.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Escape the Starting House">
    Upon spawning, you'll be in a house with its downstairs flooded. Your only exit is through a downstairs window leading to the ocean. Grab any available items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Boat Across the Ocean">
    Four boats are usually found near the starting house. Choose one and begin traveling between the other houses to gather supplies like **Flare Guns**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Follow the Lighthouse">
    Eventually, fog will set in, and a lighthouse on the final house will activate. Follow its light to reach this house.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enter the Shark Tank">
    Inside the final house, locate four **Diving Helmets** next to a shark tank. Equip one and enter the tank.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Find the Underwater Cave">
    Once in the ocean, **The Thing on Level 7** will attack the tank, forcing you to swim. Search for a lit cave entrance within a rock structure at the ocean floor. Enter it to reach Level 8.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Beware The Thing">
During the underwater descent, **The Thing on Level 7** will actively pursue you. Be prepared for a sudden encounter as it breaks the shark tank, forcing you into the open water.
</Callout>

The houses scattered across the ocean provide vital resources. Prioritize exploring them before the fog rolls in to maximize your chances of survival.

| Item Type     | Location        | Purpose                                |

| **Almond Water** | Various houses  | Restores health/sanity                 |
| **Juice**     | Various houses  | Provides a minor boost                 |
| **Flare Guns**| Early houses    | Utility, potentially for signaling/distraction |
| **Diving Helmets** | Final house     | Essential for underwater breathing     |
