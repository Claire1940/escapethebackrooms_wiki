<Section icon={Target} title="Solving the Initial Counting Puzzle">
The first major hurdle in Escape the Backrooms Level 4 is a unique counting puzzle in the initial room. The numbers for this puzzle are randomized with each playthrough, meaning you'll need to actively count the items yourself.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Water Pumps">
    Upon entering the first room, immediately look for water pumps. Count every pump you find. This number will be one of the inputs for the puzzle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Count the Books">
    Next, systematically scan the room for books. Ensure you count all visible books, as they can be scattered on shelves, desks, or even on the floor. Input this total into the puzzle interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Tally the Chairs">
    Proceed to count all the chairs in the room. Be thorough; sometimes chairs can be partially obscured or tucked away in corners.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Identify the Tables">
    Finally, count the tables within the room. Once you have all four numbers (pumps, books, chairs, tables), input them into the designated puzzle mechanism.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Puzzle Randomization Alert">
The exact numbers for the water pumps, books, chairs, and tables **change with every playthrough**. Do not rely on previous attempts or external guides for the specific counts. You must perform the counting yourself each time.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Camera} title="Navigating the Camera Surveillance System">
After successfully solving the counting puzzle and gathering resources, you'll encounter a corridor with a camera surveillance system. This requires a stealthy approach to avoid detection. Getting caught will force you back to a reset switch, costing valuable time.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Camera Detection">
    - **Trigger**: Being seen by any of the four cameras.
    - **Consequence**: The final exit door will close, requiring a reset.
    - **Avoidance**: Stay out of their direct line of sight.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reset Mechanism">
    - **Location**: A dedicated reset button found after the puzzle room.
    - **Function**: Reopens the final door if you are detected.
    - **Strategy**: Learn the camera patterns to avoid needing the reset.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="info" title="Camera Reset Strategy">
If you are spotted by a camera, immediately backtrack to the reset camera switch. It's better to reset early than to struggle further down the corridor with the exit closed. Learn the camera routes to anticipate their movements.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Dodge the First Camera">
    As you enter the camera area, the first camera will be visible. Follow the route to the left, hugging the wall to bypass it without being seen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bypass the Second Camera">
    The second camera is typically positioned to monitor a wider area. Walk around any obstacles or shelves, ensuring you do not enter areas that would expose you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate the Third and Fourth Cameras">
    These cameras are often positioned in a hallway of doors. Turn right at the appropriate junction and continue through the corridor, making sure to stay out of their view.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach the Exit Door">
    Once past the cameras, continue through the final set of doors. The exit will be at the very end of this route.
  </Step>
</Steps>
