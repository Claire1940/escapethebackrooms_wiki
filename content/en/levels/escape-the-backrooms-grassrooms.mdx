### Part 1: The First Rooms

Upon spawning, your initial task is to explore the immediate vicinity to locate a large room.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activate Levers">
    Locate a large room with a nearly entirely grassy ground and **two levers**. Pull both levers once to move bridge pieces across to the other side.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Cross the Bridge">
    Step onto one of the bridge pieces. It will automatically transport you to the opposite side.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Find the Knife">
    On the other side, you'll find a concrete block with ropes and a wooden plank held by a rope. Pull a lever on this concrete block to unlock a metal door below. Enter this door to find some **Knives**. Pick one up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Cut the Rope">
    Return to the concrete block. Use the Knife to cut the rope holding the wooden plank.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Advance to the Next Area">
    Walk across the now-freed plank to reach the next room. Ascend the staircase within this room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Open the Path">
    At the top of the staircase, cut another rope. This will cause a wood piece to drop onto glass below, opening the next area for you to proceed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
