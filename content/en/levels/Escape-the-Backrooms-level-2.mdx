<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough for Level 2">
Successfully navigating Level 2 requires a clear understanding of the sequence of events. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth passage and escape the Backrooms.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Entry and Exploration">
    Upon entering Level 2, you'll find yourself in a long hallway. Utilize the **Bunny Hop** trick (Shift + Space) to traverse this section quickly. Keep an eye out for a flashlight, which is usually located in a slightly darker spot along the path. Equip it immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigating Dark Zones and Steam">
    After the initial hallway, you will enter a dark area. Your flashlight is essential here. As you proceed, you may encounter steam vents. Carefully **crouch** or sprint through these hazards. Listen for audio cues, as a distinct sound often precedes a steam burst.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Smiler Chase Initiation">
    Roughly halfway through the dark section, you will hear a distinct noise or see a text prompt indicating the presence of an entity. This is the **Smiler**, and it will begin chasing you. At this point, your stamina becomes infinite, so focus solely on running forward without stopping.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Escape Route Identification">
    Continue running at full speed. You will eventually see a brick wall to your left. This is a dead end. The actual escape route is a hole or opening on your left, slightly past the brick wall. Quickly enter this opening.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reaching Level 3">
    Once you enter the opening, the door behind you will close, signaling the end of the Smiler chase and Level 2. You will then arrive at Level 3, the power station.
  </Step>
</Steps>
