<Section icon={Target} title="Navigating the Neighborhood: Objectives and Threats">
Your journey in the Neighborhood begins by locating a virtual map near the metal gate of the abandoned M.E.G. base. This map will highlight several houses in red, indicating your targets. Your main goal is to enter these flashing houses and activate something on the computers found within. There are typically three computers you need to interact with.

The Neighborhood is not without its dangers. A formidable entity known as the **Neighborhood Watch** patrols the roads, characterized by its extreme speed. If detected, your best course of action is to quickly seek refuge inside a nearby house and secure the door. Inside the houses, you'll encounter **Wretches**. Fortunately, most doors within the houses have locks, which you can use to prevent Wretches from pursuing you.

<Callout type="warning" title="Entity Encounters">
The Neighborhood Watch is incredibly fast and will chase you relentlessly. Your only defense is to break line of sight and secure yourself within a house. Wretches inside houses can be locked out, but be quick and decisive.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Strategy for Computer Activation and Safe Houses">
Activating the three computers requires a methodical approach. The game often generates houses with varying levels of danger. Some houses might be "safe," meaning they won't contain Wretches, offering a free computer activation. Others will be "dangerous," hosting Wretches that you'll need to evade or lock away.

It's highly recommended to prioritize any house marked as "safe" on your virtual map first. This guarantees one computer activation without the immediate threat of a Wretch. While luck plays a role in how many safe houses you receive, typically at least one is provided.

<Section icon={Swords} title="M.E.G. Base Access and Progression">
Once all three computers in the Neighborhood houses are activated, the metal gate to the abandoned M.E.G. base will unlock. Head inside and follow the arrows painted on the walls, which will guide you to the basement. This marks the transition from the open-ended exploration of the Neighborhood to a more linear path within the M.E.G. base.

The M.E.G. base section, often referred to as the Abandoned Outpost, has its own set of challenges and objectives. Upon successful completion of this outpost, you will acquire a keycard. This keycard is crucial for your final exit from Level 9. Proceed to the back gate of the M.E.G. base and use the keycard at the terminal to open it, granting you access to Level 10.

<Callout type="success" title="Path to Level 10">
Successfully navigating the M.E.G. base and obtaining the keycard is the final hurdle in Level 9. The keycard terminal at the back gate is your gateway to the next level of the Backrooms.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Tips for Surviving Escape the Backrooms Level 9">
Level 9 can be challenging, but with the right tactics, you can navigate it successfully. Here are some essential tips to help you survive and progress:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Map Awareness">
    Constantly refer to your virtual map to track flashing houses and plan your route. Avoid aimless wandering, as this increases your chances of encountering the Neighborhood Watch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Door Management">
    Inside houses, always be aware of doors. Use locks immediately if a Wretch is detected or if you want to secure a room while activating a computer. Doors are your primary defense against Wretches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stealth and Speed">
    When outside, try to stay aware of your surroundings to spot the Neighborhood Watch before it spots you. If a chase begins, run directly to the nearest house and close the door. Don't try to outrun it in the open.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resourcefulness">
    While not explicitly resource-heavy, Level 9 requires quick thinking and adaptability. Be prepared for unexpected Wretch appearances or sudden encounters with the Neighborhood Watch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Patience">
    Rushing through the Neighborhood can lead to mistakes. Take your time to observe entity patterns and secure objectives methodically. Patience is key to successful navigation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
