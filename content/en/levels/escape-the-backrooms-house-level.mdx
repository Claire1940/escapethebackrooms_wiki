<Section icon={Shield} title="Surviving the Night: Curfew and Hiding Mechanics">

The most critical mechanic in the **escape the backrooms house level** is the day/night cycle. The level operates on a strict schedule announced by massive sirens playing distorted 1900s-style cartoon music. When the music stops, the "King's Curfew" begins.

During the night, hordes of Animations—hostile, cartoonish entities—spawn across the hills. If you are caught outside, your chances of survival are near zero. Even inside a house, you are not entirely safe; Animations can break down doors if they detect your presence. To survive, you must use specific hiding spots found within the houses.

| Time Period | Duration | Safety Level | Player Action Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Initial Day** | 3 Minutes | Safe | Looting and moving toward the Castle |
| **Subsequent Days** | 2 Minutes | Safe | Rapid movement between houses |
| **Nighttime** | 1 Minute | Dangerous | Hiding under beds or in closets |

<Callout type="warning" title="Entity Alert">
Animations are highly aggressive. If you hear a door break while hiding, stay perfectly still. They will search the room but usually won't find you if you are tucked deep under a bed or inside a closet.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Objectives: The Path to the Animated Kingdom">

Escaping this level requires a multi-stage approach. You cannot simply walk to the end; the distance is too vast to cover on foot during a single daylight cycle. You must strategically use the trucks and navigate the neighborhood house by house.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Starting Sign">
    Upon spawning, locate the sign pointing right. Read the attached note for a warning about the curfew. This establishes your primary direction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilizing Trucks">
    Locate the trucks parked on the streets. Entering a truck will cause it to automatically drive toward the Castle. This is the fastest way to gain distance, but be aware that trucks stop and lock their doors immediately when night falls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Castle Ascent">
    Once you reach the end of the suburban expanse, you will see the floating Animated Kingdom. Locate the long rope hanging from the base of the castle and climb it to enter the next phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Funhouse Puzzle">
    Inside the castle, you must solve a shape-matching puzzle. Push the four cubes (Star, Circle, Square, and Multi-shape) onto their corresponding floor pressure plates to unlock the boss arena.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Note on Trucks">
Trucks do not have infinite fuel or range. They will take you a significant distance, but you will eventually need to finish the journey on foot or wait for another transport opportunity.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Boss Battle: Defeating the Animated King">

The final challenge of Level 94 is the Animated King. This boss battle takes place in a miniature amusement park setting. Unlike traditional combat, this is a test of survival and "red light, green light" mechanics.

**Phase 1: The Disappearing Act**
When you first approach the podium, the King is visible. Once you look away, he disappears, and the lights cut out. The battle officially begins.

**Phase 2: Survival (100 Seconds)**
You must survive for 1 minute and 40 seconds. The King will attempt to attack you from the darkness. The key mechanic is **visual contact**. If you are looking at the King, he freezes and eventually disappears to reposition. If you look away, he moves rapidly toward you.

| Mechanic | Detail | Survival Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Visual Freeze** | Looking at the King stops his movement | Keep your flashlight centered and scan 360 degrees |
| **Liquid Pain** | Dispensers spawn hazards periodically | Stay in the center of the arena to avoid spills |
| **Timer** | 100 Seconds total duration | Use your watch to track the remaining time |
| **Exit Trigger** | Roller Coaster activation | Only spawns after the timer hits zero |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Do not stop moving. Even while looking at the King, minor adjustments in your positioning help you avoid the Liquid Pain that periodically spawns on the floor.
</Callout>

</Section>
