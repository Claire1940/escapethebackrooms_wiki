<Section icon={Target} title="Navigating the Death Slides Puzzle">
The core of the **escape the backrooms overgrowth** experience revolves around solving the Death Slides puzzle. This involves locating four specific Explorers scattered throughout the maze and leading them to the slides.

<Callout type="tip" title="Explorer Interaction">
To pick up an Explorer, press 'F' (PC). You will drop them if you sprint or press 'F' again. Entities will not harm the Explorers, allowing you to drop them and run if spotted.
</Callout>

### Entity Encounters: Howlers

| Entity Name | Behavior | Threat Level | Avoidance Strategy |
| :---------- | :------- | :----------- | :----------------- |
| **Howler (Bacteria)** | Patrols, screams when spotted | High | Stay out of sight, use corners, sprint only when necessary |
| **Explorer** | Passive, must be guided | None | Pick up with 'F', drop if chased |

### Step-by-Step Explorer Retrieval

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="First Explorer (Easiest)">
    - Head to the **left side** of the map from the starting area.
    - This Explorer is usually in a less patrolled area, making it safer to retrieve.
    - Guide them to any of the four Death Slides. An animation will play, and the slide's "DANGER" sign will turn off.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Second Explorer (Right Side)">
    - Circuit over to the **right side** of the map, being cautious of entities.
    - Navigate up any stairs you encounter (marked with red bars on some maps).
    - Collect this Explorer and return them to an active Death Slide.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Third Explorer (Central Maze)">
    - This Explorer is deeper within the labyrinth, making encounters more likely.
    - Choose either the left, right, or middle path. The perimeter is often safer.
    - Be prepared to drop the Explorer and hide if a Howler spots you. Wait for them to leave before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Fourth Explorer (Most Dangerous)">
    - The final Explorer is typically in the most heavily patrolled areas.
    - Always have Almond Water ready, as your sanity will be low.
    - Use landmarks to navigate and be ready to sprint, pick up, and drop the Explorer quickly if needed for speed or evasion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Surviving **escape the backrooms overgrowth** demands more than just finding Explorers; it requires effective resource management and smart evasion tactics.

<Callout type="success" title="Almond Water is Key">
Always keep a couple of Almond Water bottles on you. Your sanity drains continuously in Overgrowth, and low sanity can impair your vision and increase entity aggression. Almond Water spawns randomly near the slides, so grab it when you see it.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Exiting Overgrowth and Reaching The Grassrooms">
Once all four Explorers have been guided into the Death Slides, the "DANGER" signs above each slide will turn off, and they will all display "ON" in green. This signifies that the path to The Grassrooms is now open.

<Callout type="info" title="Final Step">
After disarming all four Death Slides, you can safely go down any of them. Instead of dying, you will be transported to The Grassrooms, the next level in **Escape the Backrooms**.
</Callout>

### Progression Path

| Action | Outcome | Next Destination |
| :----- | :------ | :--------------- |
| **Slide is Armed** | Player death by fan | Game Over |

| **Slide disarmed by Player** | Player sent to Bone Thief's area (guaranteed death) | Game Over |
| **All 4 slides disarmed by Explorers** | All slides "ON", safe passage | **The Grassrooms** |
