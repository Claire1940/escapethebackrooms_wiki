<Section icon={Target} title="Objectives and Survival in the Backrooms Neighborhood">
Your main goal in the neighborhood segment of Level 9 is straightforward: activate three computers. However, achieving this is far from simple, given the hostile entities and the need for careful exploration. Each flashing red house on your virtual map signifies a computer that needs interaction.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Virtual Map">
    Approach the metal gate in front of the abandoned M.E.G. base. A virtual map will appear, showing the layout of the neighborhood and marking your target houses with flashing red indicators.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Target Houses">
    Use the virtual map to pinpoint the locations of the flashing red houses. Plan your route carefully to minimize exposure to the Neighborhood Watch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enter and Activate Computers">
    Enter each target house one by one. Inside, locate the computer and activate it. Be prepared for potential Wretch encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Wretch Encounters">
    Upon entering a house, immediately look for locks on doors. Use these to prevent Wretches from reaching you while you activate the computer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evade the Neighborhood Watch">
    If the Neighborhood Watch spots you while outside, run into the nearest house and quickly close the door behind you. Its speed makes direct confrontation impossible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Wretch Alert">
Wretches are a constant threat inside houses. Always prioritize locking doors behind you to create a temporary safe zone while you interact with computers.
</Callout>

Successfully activating all three computers is your ticket to the next phase of Level 9. Once done, the gate to the M.E.G. base will unlock, allowing you to proceed.
