The outdoor segment of Level 94 is a race against time and a test of your environmental awareness. As you spawn, your immediate goal is to locate the "Castle" and a rope that grants access to it. This path is straightforward, but the real challenge lies in surviving the intermittent night phases.

To survive the night, you must quickly find one of the many houses scattered across the map. These houses offer temporary sanctuary from the Animators. Once inside, securing your position is paramount.

  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Castle Rope">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Safe Houses">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prepare for Nightfall">
    Every few minutes, the environment shifts to night, and Animators will begin to spawn. A visual cue, such as the sky darkening rapidly, will indicate the impending curfew.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Seek Immediate Shelter">
    When night starts, rush to the nearest house. Once inside, immediately close the door behind you. This is a critical step to prevent Animators from entering.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Hide in a Wardrobe">
    Inside the house, locate a wardrobe, typically found near the stairs. Enter it and remain hidden until the night cycle passes. Ensure all lights are off to avoid detection.
  </Step>
</Steps>
