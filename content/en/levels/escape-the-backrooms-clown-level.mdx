<Section icon={Map} title="Roller Coaster Activation: The Path to Escape">
The ultimate goal of the Clown Level is to activate the roller coaster, which transports you to the next stage or potentially out of the Backrooms. This process involves a series of specific actions and requires careful execution while dealing with the Animated King.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Roller Coaster">
    Explore the level to find the roller coaster area. This is usually a distinct landmark within the chaotic environment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Solve the Figure Puzzle">
    Move all figures to their corresponding podiums within the Castle area. This step is crucial for opening the path to the roller coaster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain Clown Vision">
    After the door opens, keep your eyes on the Clown. It's vital to never lose sight of it during this critical phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Sanity and Items">
    Stand near the roller coaster. If your sanity is low, drink 1 almond water. Ensure you have a flashlight in your main hand and at least one almond water in your pocket.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Initiate Roller Coaster Sequence">
    Look away from the Clown for a few seconds, then look back. When the lights go off, start spinning your camera horizontally. This technique helps prevent the Clown from attacking. Once the roller coaster is ready, press "F" to activate it and escape the level.
  </Step>
</Steps>
