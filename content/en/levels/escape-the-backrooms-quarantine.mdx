Upon entering Level 9, resist the urge to explore random houses. Your first priority should be to orient yourself and find the center of the map. This central area often serves as a safe zone for item replenishment and acts as a respawn point if you perish. The map will display red dots indicating the houses containing the necessary computers. <Section icon={Target} title="Computer Acquisition & Entity Evasion">

Each house marked with a red dot on your map will contain a computer you need to interact with. However, these houses are also home to aggressive entities that will hunt you down. Successfully downloading data from these computers is essential, and once completed, it acts as a checkpoint. This means if you die after a successful download, you won't need to re-download from that specific computer.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Target House">
    Use your map to identify the nearest red-dotted house. Plan your approach carefully, keeping an eye out for the **Neighborhood Watch** entity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter and Search for the Computer">
    Once inside, move stealthily. Crouching can reduce your detection radius. Computers can be on any floor, sometimes in easily accessible spots, other times in more challenging locations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Download and Evade">
    Press 'F' to start the download. This process takes time, and entities inside the house will actively pursue you. If an entity spots you, try to create distance, use obstacles, or retreat to a safe area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Checkpoints">
    Once the download is complete, that computer is registered. If you die, you won't lose progress on that specific house. Move to the next red-dotted house.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Entity Behavior">
Entities inside houses are relentless. While some may get 'stuck' or exhibit glitched behavior allowing temporary evasion, assume they will chase you. Always have an escape route planned, even if it means running to a different room or out of the house.
</Callout>

### Dealing with the Neighborhood Watch

The **Neighborhood Watch** is a formidable entity in Level 9. This large, one-eyed monster patrols the entire suburb and is incredibly fast. If it spots you, outrunning it is nearly impossible.

| Aspect                | Description                                                               | Countermeasures                                            |
| :-------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :--------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Appearance**        | Large, one-eyed creature, often described as spider-like.                 | Listen for heavy footsteps or vibrations, indicating proximity. |
| **Speed**             | Extremely fast, capable of covering large distances quickly.             | Do not attempt to outrun it if spotted; focus on evasion.    |
| **Detection**         | Wide field of vision.                                                     | Stay out of its line of sight, use buildings for cover.      |
| **Patrol Route**      | Randomly roams the entire Darkened Suburbs map.                           | Observe its movement patterns from a safe distance.          |

<Callout type="info" title="Hub Door Location">
From the starting cave, instead of heading towards the middle, enter the house to your right. Follow a small loop inside, and you'll find the Hub door. This is a crucial location for managing your sanity.
</Callout>
