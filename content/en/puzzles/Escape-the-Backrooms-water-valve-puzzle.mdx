This section is notorious for its length and the presence of Gitoan entities.

  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Right Side Valves">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Circumvent Gitoan">
    Circle around to avoid Gitoan detection. They often patrol in specific patterns. If spotted, try to use water as a temporary refuge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete Remaining Valves">
    Systematically turn the remaining valves, always being mindful of Gitoan's proximity. In multiplayer, assign players to different sides for efficiency.
