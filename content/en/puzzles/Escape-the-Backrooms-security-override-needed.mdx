Level 1 is broken down into several distinct phases, each with its own set of objectives and challenges. Successfully navigating these stages is key to reaching the security override point.

| Phase | Objective | Key Items/Actions | Entities Encountered |

<Section icon={Target} title="Obtaining the Escape the Backrooms Security Override Needed">

To get the **security override needed** for the gate on Floor 4, you must temporarily leave Level 1 and enter The Hub. This is a critical step that requires you to locate a specific exit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Floor 4">
    Progress through Floor 1, 2, and 3 by solving their respective puzzles (color-coded elevator, finding 4 keys, locating the elevator) until you reach Floor 4. Floor 4 is characterized by rougher concrete walls and a closed gate blocking your path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate The Hub Door">
    On Floor 4, look for a door on the **left side of the room**. This door, often marked with a fire exit sign, will lead you to The Hub. It's designed to be fairly simple to find.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enter The Hub and Access M.E.G. Base">
    Once you pass through the door, you will enter The Hub. Your objective here is to find The M.E.G. Base. Within this base, you will find several computers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate the Security Override">
    Interact with the **Computers** in The M.E.G. Base. This action will grant you the necessary security override to flip the switch back in Level 1, Floor 4.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Return to Level 1 and Open the Gate">
    Go back through The Hub Door to Level 1, Floor 4. You can now approach the closed gate and flip the switch to open it, allowing you to proceed to the final section of Level 1.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="The Hub Access">
The Hub door in Floor 4 is the first Level Door in The Hub that can be unlocked. It's essential for progression, so do not miss it. The Hub itself is a central area connecting various levels.
</Callout>
