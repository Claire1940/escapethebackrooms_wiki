<Section icon={Target} title="Level Two: The Habitable Zone Elevator Keypad Code">
After surviving Level 0, players will reach the Habitable Zone, also known as Level Two. This level presents an immediate challenge: a locked elevator with a multicolored keypad. Unlike fixed codes, this elevator's sequence is randomized for every playthrough, demanding careful observation.

<Callout type="warning" title="Randomized Code Alert">
The elevator keypad code in Level Two is **randomized** for each player and each new game session. Do not rely on codes found online or from previous playthroughs; you must find it within your current game.
</Callout>

The solution lies in a smaller parking space accessible through a red door within the Habitable Zone. Inside, you'll find several cars of different colors. The order in which these cars are parked, from left to right, dictates the color sequence for the elevator keypad.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Elevator">
    Upon entering the Habitable Zone, you'll find the locked elevator to your left, next to the ventilation ducts you emerged from.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find the Red Door">
    Explore the surrounding area for a red door. This door leads to the car parking area where the code is hidden.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Identify Car Color Order">
    Enter the smaller parking space. Observe the colors of the cars parked from the left row to the right row. This sequence of colors is your elevator code.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Input the Code">
    Return to the elevator keypad. Press the colored buttons in the exact order you observed the cars. Once correct, the elevator will unlock, allowing you to proceed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Teamwork Advantage">
