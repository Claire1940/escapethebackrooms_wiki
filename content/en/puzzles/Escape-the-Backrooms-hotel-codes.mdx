<Section icon={Swords} title="Part 1: Navigating the Main Hall">

Upon entering Level 5, you are greeted by the Main Hall. Your primary objective here is to unlock the glass elevator door, which is secured by three separate locks. Unlike later puzzles involving **Escape the Backrooms hotel codes**, this section relies on resource gathering and trading with a mysterious entity via a dumbwaiter.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Bug Spray">
    Search the side rooms and cabinets in the Main Hall to find Bug Spray. This is essential for dealing with the Male Deathmoths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Harvest Moth Jelly">
    Locate three clusters of Male Deathmoths. Use the Bug Spray to kill them; they will drop Moth Jelly. If you accidentally consume the jelly, a new moth will spawn elsewhere on the floor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use the Dumbwaiter">
    Find the dumbwaiter in the left hallway. Place one Moth Jelly inside and send it down. It will return with a Key. Repeat this process three times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock the Elevator">
    Use the three Keys on the glass door locks. Proceed down the hallway to enter the elevator, which will transport you to The Hotel floor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Entity | Location | Behavior | Counter |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Male Deathmoth** | Main Hall / Hotel | Passive until approached, drops Jelly | Bug Spray |
| **Female Deathmoth** | Boiler Room | Extremely aggressive, guards hives | Valves / Bug Spray |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Do not waste your Bug Spray on single moths if you can avoid it. Focus only on the clusters needed for the Moth Jelly to ensure you have enough spray for the later sections of the level.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Part 2: Solving the Escape the Backrooms Hotel Codes">

The Hotel floor is where the most significant puzzle of Level 5 resides. To exit this floor and enter the Boiler Room, you must unlock Room 235 using a 5-digit code. While the code is randomized between three specific combinations, the method to reveal it remains consistent.

#### The Mailbox Puzzle
In the spawn room of the Hotel floor, you will find a large set of mailboxes. Several of these boxes will be open. You must visit the specific room numbers corresponding to the open boxes to collect papers.

| Action | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Identify Rooms** | Check which 7 mailboxes are open in the lobby. |
| **Search Rooms** | Go to each numbered room and pick up the paper on the dresser. |
| **Return Papers** | Place the papers back into their respective mailboxes. |
| **Reveal Code** | Look at the ceiling above the mailboxes once all 7 are placed. |

#### Valid Keypad Codes
If you are looking to skip the search or if the ceiling text is glitched, you can try the three known combinations for the Room 235 lock.

| Code Option | Sequence | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Option 1** | **17564** | Common |
| **Option 2** | **05938** | Common |
| **Option 3** | **89472** | Common |

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Solved">
Once you input one of these **Escape the Backrooms hotel codes** into the keypad at Room 235, the door will unlock, granting you access to the stairs leading down to the Boiler Room.
</Callout>
